The Activities

4 Challenges. One Brand.

Join one — or all four! Every challenge helps connect the TLS EMEA team and build visibility for what we do together.

1
Posters &
Stickers

Download a poster or grab a sticker, place it anywhere creative — your office or home desk — and share your best photo in the Slack channel.

  • Get poster or sticker from the Box link in Slack
  • Place it somewhere creative & take a photo
  • Post your photo in #tls-emea-marketing-week-2
🏆 Best photo wins a prize!
2
Market GM
Challenge

Convince your Market GM to record and share a TLS message with the entire market — showcasing key offerings or a success story. Creativity wins!

  • Pitch your best TLS story to your Market GM
  • GM records & shares a short video message
  • Market sees TLS in action — visibility grows!
🏆 Best GM message wins a special prize!
3
TLS Insight
Session

Set up a meeting with colleagues outside of TLS — from other brands or the Horizon/Select sales team. Online or face-to-face, 10–15 minutes is all it takes.

  • Schedule a short meeting outside TLS
  • Explain what TLS does & the value we bring
  • Post a screenshot or selfie in Slack to win
💡 Blue Points scale with attendees!
4
LinkedIn
Challenge

Create visibility on LinkedIn using pre-made templates (in Box) or your own content — within IBM guidelines. Every post with #IAMTLS enters you into the prize pool.

  • Get LinkedIn templates from Box via Slack
  • Post TLS content with #IAMTLS on LinkedIn
  • Each post = 1 ticket in the prize draw!
🎟 Prize pool drawing — every post counts!
